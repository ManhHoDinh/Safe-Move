### Section 1: Hero

**Ten section:** Hero -- Man hinh dau tien

**Headline:**

> "Moi con duong ngap la mot quyet dinh thanh pho cua ban chua bao gio dua ra."

**Subheadline:**

SafeMove AI thu thap du lieu tu camera giao thong, he thong thoi tiet, du lieu lich su va tin tuc truc tuyen de phat hien ngap lut do thi theo thoi gian thuc -- sau do dinh tuyen lai toan bo mang luoi giao thong truoc khi chiec xe dau tien cham mat nuoc.

**CTA Chinh:** `[ Yeu cau truy cap som ]`
Nen Storm Blue (#2563EB), kich thuoc xl (56px chieu cao), chu trang, bo tron lg, phat sang nhe khi hover, hieu ung pulse nhe khi idle.

**CTA Phu:** `[ Xem Demo -> ]`
Nut ghost, vien trang 1px, chu trang voi icon play, mo video overlay inline.

**Thong diep cot loi (Narrative):**

Hero phai ngay lap tuc loai bo gia dinh rang day la mot ung dung dieu huong khac. Khung thong diep la *tri tue ha tang*. Nguoi xem phai cam nhan rang SafeMove AI la he than kinh con thieu ma cac thanh pho can khi mua bien mang luoi duong do thi thanh vung nguy hiem. Su khan cap mang tinh bau khong khi -- khong phai hoang loan, ma la su binh tinh quyen uy cua phong chi huy da biet dieu gi sap xay ra.

**Huong dan hinh anh:**

- Nen toi toan viewport (#0A0E1A). Ban do ve tinh toi (Mapbox Dark style hoac vector tiles tuy chinh o z14 zoom) chiem toan bo viewport o 40% opacity phia sau van ban hero.
- Ba den bon vung ngap render duoi dang gradient dong tam do-do tham, phat xung moi 2 giay voi dao dong opacity 0.3.
- Mot duong polyline sang cyan-sang-xanh la hoat hinh tu diem xuat phat den dich, ro rang chuyen huong tranh cac vung do theo thoi gian thuc.
- Hieu ung hat doc theo duong tuyen goi y luong du lieu.
- Goc trai tren: wordmark SafeMove AI toi gian, trang. Goc phai tren: nut `Yeu cau truy cap som`.
- Lop phu texture mua hoat hinh rat nhe o 5% opacity tren ban do, thuan tuy tao khong khi.
- Ban do phai cam thay song -- mot giao dien van hanh dang hoat dong, khong phai minh hoa tinh.

### Section 3: Giai phap

**Ten section:** Giai phap -- Bon tru cot nang luc

**Headline:**

> "Bon tru cot. Mot su menh: Duong pho biet nghi truoc khi ban lai."

**Subheadline:**

SafeMove AI van hanh vong lap tri tue lien tuc -- phat hien su kien ngap, danh gia muc do, tinh toan tuyen duong an toan, va hoc hoi tu moi ket qua.

**4 Tru cot nang luc (4 cot):**

---

#### Tru cot 1: PHAT HIEN

**Goi y icon:** Xung radar / vong dong tam (Lucide: `radio` hoac icon sonar tuy chinh)
**Nhan:** Phat hien ngap thoi gian thuc

Thu nhan du lieu cam bien truc tiep, hinh anh camera giao thong, canh bao ngap chinh phu, radar thoi tiet, va crawl mang xa hoi cung tin tuc lien tuc tim tu khoa ngap gan vi tri den cac doan duong. Khong nguon du lieu don le nao duoc tin tuong don doc -- he thong doi chieu cheo qua nhieu kenh truoc khi danh dau. Khi muc nuoc tren duong Nguyen Huu Canh dang 15cm luc 3 gio sang, he thong biet trong vong 90 giay.

---

#### Tru cot 2: DANH GIA

**Goi y icon:** Dong ho do / muc do nghiem trong (Lucide: `gauge` hoac icon muc nuoc nhieu tang)
**Nhan:** Danh gia muc do bang AI

LLM tinh chinh danh gia moi diem ngap phat hien so voi nguong kha nang di qua cua phuong tien. Xem xet uoc tinh do sau nuoc, hinh hoc duong, ha tang thoat nuoc, va lich su ngap dai dang tai chinh xac vi tri do de gan xep hang muc do: Di duoc, Can trong, Anh huong, hoac Bi chan. LLM hieu ngu canh: "nuoc ngap ngang bap chan tren Nguyen Hue" khac can ban voi "he thong thoat nuoc qua tai tren cao toc Quan 7."

---

#### Tru cot 3: DINH TUYEN

**Goi y icon:** Duong re nhanh / nga ba (Lucide: `route` hoac `git-branch` xoay)
**Nhan:** Dinh tuyen toi uu nhan biet ngap

Dong co dinh tuyen hoc tang cuong tinh toan lai duong di toi uu theo thoi gian thuc, tich hop du lieu muc do ngap voi dieu kien giao thong truc tiep qua Google Maps API. Tuyen duong khong chi "tranh vung ngap" -- chung duoc toi uu toan cuc cho thoi gian di chuyen, bien an toan, va dung luong duong duoi ap luc. Khi phat hien ngap tren Vo Van Kiet, tuyen thay the duoc tinh trong 1.2 giay.

---

#### Tru cot 4: HOC HOI

**Goi y icon:** Nao voi vong phan hoi / mach than kinh (Lucide: `brain-circuit`)
**Nhan:** He thong hoc lien tuc

Moi chuyen di duoc dinh tuyen tao ra phan hoi. Xac nhan tai xe, lech quy dao GPS, va bao cao ngap hau su kien huan luyen he thong agent cai thien do chinh xac phat hien, hieu chuan muc do, va chat luong tuyen duong theo thoi gian. He thong sau su kien ngap thu 100 sac ben hon can ban so voi sau su kien dau tien. Sau mot mua mua, do chinh xac tang tu 89% len 94.7%.

---

**Huong dan hinh anh:**

- Bo cuc chia doi: nua trai hien thi anh giao lo ngap hon loan (giam bao hoa, overlay do 20%), nua phai hien thi cung giao lo tren ban do SafeMove AI voi vung muc do sach se va tuyen khuyen nghi ve bang xanh la.
- Bon tru cot nam duoi chia doi duoi dang cot the, nen toi, vien tren to mau theo chu de (xanh duong cho Phat hien, cam cho Danh gia, xanh la cho Dinh tuyen, tim cho Hoc hoi).
- Khi hover, moi the nang nhe (translateY -4px) va icon hoat hinh -- radar phat xung, dong ho do day, tuyen duong ve, nao phat tia.

### Section 4: Cach hoat dong

**Ten section:** Quy trinh -- Bon buoc tu tin hieu tho den dich an toan

**Headline:**

> "Tu tin hieu tho den den noi an toan trong bon buoc."

**Subheadline:**

Duong ong tri tue lien tuc khong bao gio ngung quan sat, khong bao gio ngung hoc.

**4 Buoc (timeline ngang voi node ket noi):**

---

**Buoc 1: Thu thap du lieu**

*Mau node:* Slate Blue (#475569)

Hop nhat du lieu da nguon van hanh 24/7. Camera giao thong phan tich qua computer vision. Cam bien ngap IoT kiem tra moi 30 giay. Radar thoi tiet moi 5 phut. Tin tuc va mang xa hoi crawl lien tuc tim tu khoa ngap gan vi tri den doan duong. API canh bao chinh phu giam sat thoi gian thuc. Khong co trang thai "tat."

*Hinh anh: Cum icon nguon du lieu (camera, ve tinh, cam bien, bao chi, API) do vao node trung tam.*

---

**Buoc 2: Phan tich AI**

*Mau node:* Storm Blue (#2563EB)

Tang tri tue LLM xu ly moi tin hieu den. Doi chieu hanh vi ngap lich su tai moi vi tri. Danh gia dung luong thoat nuoc, cao do duong, va dong chay nuoc thuong nguon. Gan phan loai muc do voi diem tin cay. Danh dau mau thuan giua cac nguon de xem xet khi tin cay giam duoi nguong. Tin hieu mau thuan kich hoat dieu tra, khong phai triet tieu.

*Hinh anh: Node xu ly trung tam voi nhanh phan tich toa ra. Badge tin cay hoat hinh tu 0% len 94%.*

---

**Buoc 3: Dinh tuyen thong minh**

*Mau node:* Emerald (#10B981)

Dong co dinh tuyen RL nhan trang thai ngap-giao thong truc tiep va tinh toan duong di toi uu cho tai xe ca nhan va phoi hop cap doi xe. Tich hop du lieu giao thong Google Maps, vung muc do ngap, dung luong duong khi giam lan, va du doan tien trien ngap trong 1-6 gio toi. Tuyen duong cap nhat dong khi dieu kien thay doi.

*Hinh anh: Node ban do hien thi tuyen tinh lai -- duong xam mo dan, duong xanh la ve ra, badge "Tiet kiem 23 phut."*

---

**Buoc 4: Hoc lien tuc**

*Mau node:* Violet (#8B5CF6)

Du lieu hau chuyen di chay nguoc vao he thong. Vet GPS tiet lo tai xe co di theo tuyen khuyen nghi hay lech (va tai sao). Xac nhan cong dong kiem chung hoac sua danh gia muc do ngap. Mo phong replay mo hinh hoa dieu gi se xay ra voi quyet dinh dinh tuyen thay the. Moi diem du lieu mai sac du doan tiep theo.

*Hinh anh: Node phan hoi vong tron voi so lieu cai thien: "Do chinh xac: 89% -> 94.7%" va "Toc do dinh tuyen: 4.2s -> 1.1s."*

---

**Huong dan hinh anh:**

- Thanh timeline ngang ket noi bon node tron, moi node phat sang bang mau duoc gan.

- Duoi moi node, the mo rong khi cuon voi van ban mo ta.
- Tren mobile: chuyen sang timeline doc, node o canh trai, mo ta ben phai.
- Nen: pattern luoi rat nhe o 3% opacity goi so do ky thuat.

### Section 5: Tang tri tue AI

**Ten section:** Tang cong nghe -- Ba he thong AI phoi hop

**Headline:**

> "Ba he thong AI. Mot bo nao ngap-giao thong thong nhat."

**Subheadline:**

SafeMove AI khong phai mot mo hinh don le -- day la kien truc tri tue phoi hop noi mo hinh ngon ngu, agent tu tri, va mo phong vat ly cong tac theo thoi gian thuc.

**3 Cot:**

---

#### Cot 1: TRI TUE LLM

**Ten phu:** Danh gia ngap bang ngon ngu tu nhien

Mo hinh ngon ngu lon chuyen biet theo linh vuc, huan luyen tren tai lieu ky thuat ngap, thong so thoat nuoc do thi, va bao cao su kien ngap lich su. Doc du lieu phi cau truc -- bai bao, bai dang mang xa hoi, ban tin chinh phu -- va trich xuat tri tue ngap chinh xac: vi tri, uoc tinh do sau, thoi diem bat dau, thoi luong du kien, va tac dong den doan duong cu the. Ly luan ve muc do ngap theo cach mot ky su thanh pho ky cuu se lam, nhung o toc do may tren moi con duong dong thoi.

**Diem ky thuat:** Xu ly song song tieng Viet va tieng Anh. Fine-tuned tren 50,000+ bao cao ngap Dong Nam A. Hieu phuong ngu dia phuong trong bao cao mang xa hoi.

---

#### Cot 2: HE THONG AGENT

**Ten phu:** Phoi hop quyet dinh tu tri

Kien truc da agent noi cac agent chuyen biet xu ly trach nhiem rieng biet: Agent Phat hien xac minh bao cao ngap, Agent Muc do hieu chuan diem rui ro, Agent Dinh tuyen tinh toan duong di, va Agent Hoc hoi xu ly vong phan hoi. Agent giao tiep qua trang thai chia se va co the leo thang tinh huong mo ho. Khi tin hieu hoi tu, he thong leo thang canh bao. Khi tin hieu mau thuan, agent dieu tra them truoc khi bao dong. Day khong phai tu dong hoa. Day la phan doan o toc do may.

**Diem ky thuat:** Giao thuc phoi hop multi-agent. Bo nho ngan han va dai han. He thong escalation 3 cap: thong bao, canh bao, khan cap.

---

#### Cot 3: DONG CO MO PHONG

**Ten phu:** Mo hinh hoa ngap-giao thong du doan

Dong co mo phong dua tren vat ly mo hinh hoa dong chay nuoc, suy giam dung luong mang duong, va tai phan bo giao thong duoi dieu kien ngap. Chay kich ban Monte Carlo du doan mo rong vung ngap qua cua so 1, 3, va 6 gio. Stress-test quyet dinh dinh tuyen truoc khi gui den tai xe, dam bao tuyen khuyen nghi se khong bi ngap trong hanh trinh. Dau ra mo phong do truc tiep vao dong co dinh tuyen RL nhu rang buoc nhin-ve-phia-truoc.

**Diem ky thuat:** Mo phong len den 500,000 phuong tien dong thoi. Replay su kien ngap lich su. Xuat bao cao so sanh kich ban.

---

**Thanh so lieu duoi cung (3 metric, can giua, so lon):**

| So lieu | Gia tri | Nhan |
|---------|---------|------|
| Cua so du doan | **6 gio** | tam nhin du bao ngap-giao thong |
| Do chinh xac phat hien | **94.7%** | nhan dien diem ngap doi chieu cheo |
| Tinh toan lai tuyen | **<800ms** | cap nhat duong di sau du lieu ngap moi |

**Huong dan hinh anh:**

- Ba cot tren nen toi, moi cot co glow mau mo o tren cung (xanh duong cho LLM, tim cho Agent, teal cho Mo phong).
- Moi cot co icon hoat hinh toi gian: LLM hien phan tich van ban streaming, Agent hien node ket noi phat xung, Mo phong hien gon nuoc mo rong tren luoi.
- Thanh stat duoi dung JetBrains Mono 48px, tao cam giac dashboard du lieu ky thuat.
- Duong ngang mong phan cach cot va thanh stat.

### Section 6: Dinh tuyen thoi gian thuc

**Ten section:** Demo tuong tac -- Theo doi thanh pho tu dinh tuyen lai

**Headline:**

> "Theo doi thanh pho tu dinh tuyen lai theo thoi gian thuc."

**Subheadline:**

Day la nhung gi xay ra khi su kien ngap kich hoat luc 07:42 sang ngay thuong di lam.

**Concept demo tuong tac (hoat hinh kich ban, khong phai demo san pham live):**

**Khung 1 (0s):** Ban do thanh pho sach. Giao thong binh thuong. Tuyen duong nguoi di lam duoc highlight bang xanh la tu diem xuat phat den dich.

**Khung 2 (3s):** Canh bao ngap kich hoat tren duong huyet mach chinh. Doan bi anh huong phat xung do. Badge muc do xuat hien: "BI ANH HUONG -- Uoc tinh sau 35cm, dang dang."

**Khung 3 (5s):** Tuyen xanh la mo dan. Dong co RL tinh toan lai. Chi bao tai hien "<800ms tinh toan lai." Tuyen moi tu ve bang cyan, chuyen huong bac tranh vung ngap.

**Khung 4 (7s):** So lieu hoat hinh ben canh ban do:
- *Tuyen tinh lai trong:* 743ms
- *Thoi gian tiet kiem so voi gap ngap:* 28 phut
- *Muc do ngap tai doan tranh:* BI ANH HUONG (dang thanh BI CHAN trong ~45 phut)
- *Do tin cay tuyen thay the:* 96.2% an toan trong 2 gio toi

**Khung 5 (10s):** Vung ngap mo rong nhe (tien trien du doan hien duoi dang halo do nhat hon). Tuyen duong giu nguyen. Nhan nho: "Mo phong xac nhan tuyen kha thi den 09:30 sang."

**Hien thi so lieu (3 the metric duoi ban do):**

| So lieu | Gia tri | Ngu canh |
|---------|---------|----------|
| Thoi gian tinh toan lai | **743ms** | Tu du lieu ngap moi den cap nhat duong di |
| Thoi gian tiet kiem | **28 phut** | So voi gap ngap truc tiep |
| Do tin cay tuyen | **96.2%** | An toan cho 2 gio toi theo mo phong |

**Huong dan hinh anh:**

- Section chiem khoang 70vh.
- Ban do la trung tam, chiem 60% chieu rong desktop, the metric noi ben phai 40%.
- Dung Mapbox GL JS hoac chuoi hoat hinh pre-rendered.
- Hoat hinh tu phat khi cuon vao va phat lai qua nut "Phat lai."
- The metric dung glassmorphism card voi so monospace.

**3 The:**

---

#### The 1: DU DOAN NGAP

**Icon:** Dong ho voi giot nuoc (Lucide `clock` + element nuoc tuy chinh)

#### The 2: BAO CAO TAC DONG GIAO THONG

**Icon:** Bieu do cot voi tam giac canh bao (Lucide `bar-chart-3` + `alert-triangle`)

Bao cao tu dong luong hoa cach su kien ngap gian doan mang luoi giao thong do thi. Bao gom doan duong bi anh huong, uoc tinh gio-xe mat, mau tai phan bo un tac, va khuyen nghi dieu chinh tin hieu giao thong. Tao moi 15 phut trong su kien dang hoat dong va tom tat hau su kien.

**Tag mau dau ra:** `BAO CAO: Su kien ngap Quan 7 -- 12 doan bi anh huong -- Uoc tinh 4,200 gio-xe mat -- Un tac lan sang 3 duong huyet mach lan can -- Khuyen nghi dieu chinh pha den tai 5 giao lo`

### Section 8: Gia tri cho Chinh phu & Doanh nghiep

**Ten section:** Gia tri kep -- Xay cho hai luc luong van hanh thanh pho

**Headline:**

> "Xay cho hai luc luong van hanh thanh pho: Ha tang chinh phu va Doi xe tu nhan."

**Subheadline:**

Doi tuong khac nhau, mot tang tri tue thong nhat.

**Hai cot:**

---

#### Cot CHINH PHU (trai)

**Icon tieu de:** Toa nha / cot tru (Lucide: `landmark`)

**5 Nang luc:**

1. **Dashboard ngap-giao thong toan thanh pho** -- Giao dien van hanh thoi gian thuc cho moi doan duong duoc giam sat voi trang thai ngap, muc do, va tac dong giao thong. Thiet ke cho Trung tam Dieu hanh Khan cap.

2. **Quy hoach ha tang du doan** -- Phan tich mau ngap lich su xac dinh diem suy yeu man tinh trong mang luoi thoat nuoc va duong xa. Uu tien chi tieu von noi du lieu chung minh quan trong nhat.

3. **Canh bao khan cap tu dong** -- Day canh bao ngap-giao thong den nguoi dan qua SMS, thong bao ung dung, va tich hop voi he thong phat thanh khan cap thanh pho hien co.

4. **Phan tich hau su kien** -- Bao cao hau ngap toan dien do luong hieu qua phan ung, tac dong nguoi dan, hieu suat ha tang, va khuyen nghi cai thien.

5. **Phoi hop tin hieu giao thong** -- Khuyen nghi pha den thich ung ngap giam un tac lan toa khi duong huyet mach ngung hoat dong do ngap.

---

#### Cot DOANH NGHIEP (phai)

**Icon tieu de:** Xe tai / doi xe (Lucide: `truck`)

**4 Nang luc:**

1. **Toi uu tuyen duong doi xe** -- Dinh tuyen nhan biet ngap thoi gian thuc cho doi giao hang, logistics, va goi xe. Giam thieu cham tre, bao ve phuong tien, duy tri SLA trong su kien ngap.

2. **Canh bao an toan tai xe** -- Canh bao tuc thi day den tai xe dang tiep can vung ngap voi danh gia muc do va duong vong khuyen nghi. Giam trach nhiem phap ly va bao ve sinh mang.

3. **Tri tue rui ro chuoi cung ung** -- Danh gia tac dong ngap len tuyen tiep can kho, ket noi cang, va mang giao hang chang cuoi. Tien bo tri hang ton kho truoc gian doan du doan.

4. **Du lieu bao hiem va tuan thu** -- Nhat ky phoi nhiem ngap duoc xac minh cho khieu nai bao hiem doi xe, bao cao tuan thu quy dinh, va bao lanh rui ro.
